hello, music lover

We've been expecting you

When we experience things that move us, the next thing we want is to find others who'll share it with us.

And right now, hundreds of people are trying to share their listening experiences online.

See for yourself 🡪

And well, it's a bit awkward.

  • It's hard to refer to a specific part of a track
  • It requires leaving one app for another to listen
  • It's hard to collate and understand the collective experience of many for a given track

What if there was a better way for music lovers to share the music that moves them?
A central place, tightly integrated with your music streaming service, which gives you the tools to talk about your music accurately and share it with others.

Welcome to sheer wonder.

It's as if your personal Spotify account and Twitter had a baby

And here's how it works:

  • Connect your Spotify account to sheer wonder
  • Find a track that's special to you
  • Isolate a segment
  • Share a thought
  • Follow and interact with others who do the same. (Or keep your account private and watch)
  • Follow the tracks in your playlists to see what people are saying about them
  • Create stories - playlists that have an added layer of context, curation and storytelling around the music that makes them.

Music discovery, journalism and networking just got an upgrade.

And when you're ready to start connecting with people who inhabit the same musical spaces as you do, you know right where to find us.

Happy exploring.
